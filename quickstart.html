Download

Up and running in ~2 minutes

AeroVRC is a single portable app — there’s nothing to install and no account to make. Here’s the whole setup.

  1. 1

    Download AeroVRC

    Grab the free, portable .exe from the download page. Save it anywhere you like — your desktop, a tools folder, wherever.

  2. 2

    Run it

    Double-click AeroVRC.exe. On first launch Windows SmartScreen may say “unrecognized app” because it isn’t code-signed — click More info → Run anyway. (More on why that’s normal and what AeroVRC does with your data on the privacy page.)

  3. 3

    Start monitoring

    Click Start Monitoring at the bottom-left. AeroVRC now watches VRChat and relaunches it automatically if it crashes or closes. It detects your VRChat install on its own — just launch VRChat once and the dashboard fills in with your world, roster and frame-times.

  4. 4

    Add your companion apps Optional

    On the Apps tab, add your OSC tools, trackers or overlays by .exe path or Steam App ID, then tick Auto-launch so they open with VRChat. Group several into a one-click Preset for “full body” vs “desktop” nights.

  5. 5

    Explore the rest Optional

    Everything else is there when you want it: the live Dashboard, Performance tweaks, VRCX history, the Cache tab, bookmarks, photos and stats. See the features for the full tour.
