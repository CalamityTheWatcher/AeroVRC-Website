08 SCREENS · 1120PX CAPTURES · ZERO RETOUCH

01 / 08

01 · Dashboard

The dashboard. Monitoring status, your current world, and the live telemetry grid — uptime, in-world time, players nearby, the played-today goal ring — with hardware & tracking status underneath, always up to date.

  • Uptime & in-world time
  • Players nearby
  • Played-today goal ring
02 · Players

Players. A live who’s-here roster with join times, watchlist stars and per-player notes — export the roster or look anyone up.

  • Join times
  • Watchlist stars
  • Per-player notes
03 · Hardware

Hardware. Live sensors while VRChat runs — GPU, VRAM, temps, frame rate, ping and memory, each with a rolling 10-minute graph.

  • GPU · VRAM · temps
  • Frame rate & ping
  • 10-min rolling graphs
04 · Apps

Apps. Add your OSC tools, trackers and utilities by .exe path or Steam App ID, then let AeroVRC open them the moment monitoring starts.

  • .exe or Steam App ID
  • Auto-launch
  • SlimeVR · Virtual Desktop
05 · Presets

Presets. Group your apps into one-click sets — tick Auto-launch to open a whole preset the moment monitoring begins.

  • Group your apps
  • Auto-launch a set
  • Launch · Edit · Delete
06 · Statistics

Statistics. Playtime charts, an activity heatmap, session lengths and restart counts — with daily and weekly goals, so you can watch the watchdog earn its keep.

  • 7-day playtime chart
  • Activity heatmap
  • Sessions & restarts
07 · Settings

Settings. Deep, friendly controls — crash and break alerts, automation, hardware monitoring, integrations, bedtime/sleep, watchlists and more.

  • Crash & break alerts
  • Automation & integrations
  • Bedtime · watchlists
08 · Performance

Performance. One toggle frees up resources for VRChat by closing the apps you list — then reopens them afterwards — plus VRChat cache management.

  • Frees resources on toggle
  • Reopens apps after
  • VRChat cache controls
